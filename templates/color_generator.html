MCG

Material Color Generator

This tool is far from finished.
An external tool like ColorZilla is needed to get the color values.
The colors below 500 (400, 300,...) are exactly the ones Google used as Material Color Palettes
The ones greater than 500 are a little off from the ones Google used.
